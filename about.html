About Stree Shakti Sangathan

Empowering women. Strengthening communities. Building a just society.

Who We Are

Stree Shakti Sangathan is a registered non-profit organization established in 2022, with its headquarters in Rohtak, Haryana. We work across a wide geographical area of India, reaching women in both rural and urban communities.

Our organization is committed to creating a society where every woman lives with dignity, confidence, and equal opportunities.

Our Vision

To build a society where all women are empowered, self-reliant, aware of their rights, and free to lead their lives with dignity and respect.

Our Mission

  • To empower women through education and awareness
  • To promote skill development and self-reliance
  • To support women’s health, safety, and well-being
  • To strengthen women’s participation in social and economic life

What We Do

At Stree Shakti Sangathan, our work focuses on practical, grassroots-level initiatives that create real impact in women’s lives.

  • Women empowerment programs
  • Skill development and training initiatives
  • Health and hygiene awareness programs
  • Support for self-help groups and livelihood activities
  • Community engagement and capacity building

Who We Work With

Our programs are designed for all women, with special focus on those from underprivileged and marginalized communities. We work closely with local communities to ensure inclusive and sustainable development.

Our Values

  • Equality and respect for all
  • Dignity and empowerment of women
  • Transparency and accountability
  • Community participation and collaboration
